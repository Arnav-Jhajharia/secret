Data Analytics

  • Proficiency in data analytics provides a decisive competitive edge in various sectors, particularly in financial technology (fintech). The integration of AI with data analytics methods facilitates the leverage of process automation, enabling the efficient execution of tedious manual tasks. This comprehensive training program encompasses analytics, finance, accounting, statistics, programming, and design thinking, using real-time non-synthetic data. It will teach the learners to apply theoretical knowledge, and can be a supplement to courses already taught by the faculty. By engaging in this course, learners will be able to smoothly transition into and proactively navigate a rapidly evolving business landscape. Moreover, the course will acquaint them with popular industry tools and equip them with the necessary skills required to promptly uncover valuable insights.
